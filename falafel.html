Falafel

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Soak the chickpeas in water overnight. Drain and rinse them before using.
  2. In a food processor, blend the chickpeas, onion, parsley, cilantro, garlic, cumin, coriander, and salt until finely ground.
  3. Add the baking powder and flour, and pulse to combine. Refrigerate the mixture for at least 1 hour.
  4. Form the mixture into small balls or patties.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the falafel for 3-4 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Drain the falafel on paper towels. Serve with pita bread, tahini sauce, and salad.
