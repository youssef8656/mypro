Kabab

Ingredients

  • 500g minced lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder (optional)
  • Wooden skewers (soaked in water for 30 minutes)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the minced meat, onion, garlic, parsley, cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, pepper, and turmeric (if using).
  2. Shape the mixture into kebab patties or mold it around the soaked skewers.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Alternatively, you can grill the kababs on a barbecue.
  4. Cook the kababs on both sides for about 5-6 minutes, or until browned and cooked through.
  5. Serve with pita bread, rice, or a fresh salad. Enjoy!
