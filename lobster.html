Lobster

Ingredients

  • 2 Lobsters
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the lobsters on a baking sheet and cut them in half lengthwise.
  3. In a small bowl, mix melted butter, garlic, lemon zest, and parsley.
  4. Brush the lobster halves with the butter mixture, then season with salt and pepper.
  5. Roast the lobster for 12-15 minutes, or until the meat is opaque and tender.
  6. Serve with additional melted butter and a squeeze of lemon. Enjoy!
