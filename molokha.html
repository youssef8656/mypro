Molokhia

Ingredients

  • 500g Molokhia leaves (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, cook the chicken pieces in boiling water until tender. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, heat olive oil, then saut√© garlic and coriander until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat and add the Molokhia leaves.
  4. Simmer for about 10 minutes until the Molokhia is cooked and tender. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Shred the chicken and add it to the soup. Serve hot with rice or bread.
